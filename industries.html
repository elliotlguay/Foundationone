Industries We Serve

Specialized Maintenance for Every Commercial Sector

Different industries have different standards. We understand the specific needs, challenges, and expectations of each sector we work in.

🏛️

Condominium Corporations

Condo boards and property managers are responsible for maintaining shared exterior spaces to a standard that satisfies residents, protects property values, and meets reserve fund obligations.

Common Challenges
  • Deteriorating common-area walkways and interlock
  • Weed infiltration across shared hardscapes
  • Inconsistent maintenance from previous contractors
  • Board accountability for exterior condition
Recommended Services
  • Interlock restoration & re-leveling
  • Polymeric sanding programs
  • Seasonal walkway maintenance
  • Courtyard restoration
🏢

Property Management Companies

Property managers overseeing multi-site portfolios need a maintenance partner who can coordinate across properties, maintain consistent standards, and provide the documentation their clients expect.

Common Challenges
  • Maintaining consistent standards across sites
  • Coordinating seasonal maintenance schedules
  • Reporting to property owners and boards
  • Managing reactive vs. preventative maintenance
Recommended Services
  • Portfolio-wide maintenance programs
  • Multi-site scheduling & coordination
  • Condition reporting & documentation
  • Annual property assessments
🏥

Retirement Residences

Exterior maintenance at retirement homes carries a higher standard of care. Walkway safety, surface stability, and the cleanliness of common exterior areas directly affect resident wellbeing and family confidence.

Common Challenges
  • Uneven surfaces creating fall hazards
  • Weed growth in walking areas
  • Algae and moisture on shaded walkways
  • Maintaining appearance for prospective residents & families
Recommended Services
  • Walkway leveling & safety restoration
  • Pressure washing for algae removal
  • Courtyard maintenance programs
  • Entranceway enhancement
🛒

Commercial Plazas

Commercial plazas depend on curb appeal to attract customers and retain tenants. High-traffic hardscapes deteriorate faster and require a structured maintenance program to sustain quality and tenant satisfaction.

Common Challenges
  • Heavy foot and vehicle traffic accelerating surface wear
  • Staining and grime accumulation on entranceways
  • Weed growth in parking lot interlock
  • Managing appearance across multiple tenant fronts
Recommended Services
  • Commercial pressure washing
  • Interlock restoration & repair
  • Seasonal maintenance contracts
  • Entranceway & plaza enhancement
⚕️

Medical Facilities

Medical buildings, clinics, and healthcare campuses require a clean, professional exterior that reflects the standards of care inside. Patient and visitor first impressions begin at the parking lot and entrance.

Common Challenges
  • Maintaining high cleanliness standards outdoors
  • Accessible walkways free from hazards
  • Professional appearance year-round
  • Minimal disruption to operations during maintenance
Recommended Services
  • Pressure washing & surface cleaning
  • Walkway restoration for accessibility
  • Scheduled seasonal programs
  • Entranceway maintenance
🚗

Automotive Dealerships

Dealerships are high-visibility commercial environments where the exterior presentation directly supports the brand. Forecourts, display areas, and customer walkways need to reflect the premium standard of the vehicles being sold.

Common Challenges
  • Oil, fluid, and tire staining on forecourts
  • High-visibility surfaces affecting brand presentation
  • Heavy vehicle traffic on interlock surfaces
  • Maintaining showroom standards outdoors
Recommended Services
  • Hot water pressure washing for stain removal
  • Forecourt interlock restoration
  • Display area cleaning programs
  • Seasonal maintenance contracts
🏙️

Office Complexes

Office buildings and business parks maintain exterior spaces that communicate corporate credibility to clients, tenants, and employees. Consistent, professional maintenance is part of the building's value proposition.

Common Challenges
  • High footfall on entrance walkways
  • Maintaining consistent standards across multi-tenant buildings
  • Coordinating maintenance with tenant operations
  • Year-round curb appeal for professional image
Recommended Services
  • Walkway and entranceway maintenance
  • Polymeric sanding programs
  • Pressure washing
  • Scheduled maintenance agreements
🏭

Industrial Properties

Industrial facilities face some of the most demanding exterior maintenance conditions — heavy equipment, chemical exposure, and constant vehicle traffic all accelerate surface deterioration and create ongoing safety obligations.

Common Challenges
  • Heavy vehicle and equipment traffic on surfaces
  • Chemical and fluid contamination
  • Safety compliance for employee walkways
  • Maintaining operational continuity during maintenance
Recommended Services
  • Industrial-grade pressure washing
  • Surface restoration & repair
  • Walkway safety maintenance
  • Preventative maintenance programs
🏗️

Developers & Builders

Developers invest in high-quality interlock and hardscape during construction. Foundation One protects that investment post-completion — keeping surfaces in the condition they were designed to be in for the long term.

Common Challenges
  • Post-construction interlock settling and joint failure
  • Transitioning maintenance to new property management
  • Protecting new hardscape investment during first years
  • Deficiency identification and resolution
Recommended Services
  • Post-construction polymeric sanding
  • Interlock deficiency inspection & correction
  • Handover maintenance programs
  • Long-term surface protection plans
