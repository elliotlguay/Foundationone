Commercial Property Maintenance Services

Specialist exterior services for property managers, condo corporations, developers, and commercial organizations. Every service is designed for scale, consistency, and long-term value.

Service 01

Interlock Restoration

Over time, interlock paver surfaces shift, settle, and heave — creating uneven surfaces, gaps, and potential trip hazards. Foundation One's interlock restoration service systematically re-levels, re-aligns, and stabilizes paver surfaces to their original condition.

Overview

Complete removal and re-installation of settled or heaved paver sections. Includes base re-grading, compaction, paver re-setting, and joint restoration.

Key Benefits

  • Eliminates tripping hazards and liability exposure
  • Restores drainage and surface integrity
  • Extends the life of existing paver investment
  • Improves curb appeal and property presentation

Property Applications

  • Condo common area walkways
  • Commercial entranceways and plazas
  • Retirement home pathways
  • Office building surrounds
  • Parking lot perimeters

Service 02

Polymeric Sanding

Polymeric sand is a critical component of any well-maintained interlock surface. It locks paver joints against weed infiltration, erosion, and insect activity — and is a service that should be performed on a structured schedule.

Overview

Joint preparation, cleaning, and professional application of high-quality polymeric sand. Includes activation, compaction, and post-application inspection.

Key Benefits

  • Prevents weed and grass penetration
  • Locks pavers to resist shifting
  • Discourages insect activity in joints
  • Hardens to a durable, weather-resistant finish

Property Applications

  • All interlock paver surfaces
  • Commercial plaza walkways
  • Condo common areas
  • Patio and courtyard surfaces

Service 03

Pressure Washing

Commercial-grade pressure washing removes years of staining, biological growth, and surface contamination — restoring hardscapes and building exteriors to a clean, professional standard.

Overview

Hot and cold pressure washing for hardscapes, building facades, parking areas, entranceways, and common areas. Includes pre-treatment for stubborn staining.

Key Benefits

  • Removes algae, moss, oil, and embedded grime
  • Restores original surface colour and texture
  • Prepares surfaces for polymeric sanding or sealing
  • Immediate visual transformation

Property Applications

  • Commercial plazas and parking lots
  • Building entranceways and lobbies
  • Condo common walkways
  • Dealership forecourts
  • Industrial loading areas
