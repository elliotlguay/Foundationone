Core Services

What We Deliver

Specialized exterior maintenance services designed for commercial properties, managed communities, and professional organizations.

🧱

Interlock Restoration

Restore settled, heaved, or deteriorating interlock pavers to their original alignment and appearance.

  • Eliminates trip hazards and liability risks
  • Extends surface lifespan significantly
  • Improves property curb appeal
Walkways · Driveways · Courtyards
🪨

Polymeric Sanding

Professional joint stabilization using polymeric sand to prevent weed growth, erosion, and surface instability.

  • Prevents weed penetration through joints
  • Stabilizes pavers against shifting
  • Long-lasting protective seal
Interlock · Patio Surfaces · Plazas
💧

Pressure Washing

Commercial-grade pressure washing for hardscapes, building exteriors, and high-traffic surface areas.

  • Removes staining, algae, and grime
  • Restores surface colour and texture
  • Prepares surfaces for treatment
Parking · Entranceways · Walkways
🌿

Hardscape Weed Removal

Systematic weed elimination from interlock joints, cracks, and hardscape surfaces across managed properties.

  • Prevents structural damage from root growth
  • Maintains clean, professional appearance
  • Part of ongoing maintenance programs
All Hardscape Surfaces
